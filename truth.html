THE TRUTH OF CONVERSION THERAPY

Conversion therapy refers to attempts to change or suppress a person’s sexual orientation or gender identity. It is built on the false belief that being lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, or queer is a flaw that can be fixed. These practices range from so-called “counseling” to extreme interventions like electroshock or forced medication, though today they more often appear in religious or pseudo-psychological settings.

For years, I lived a double life. Outwardly, I tried to blend in with my friends and community. I marched at Pride, went to gay clubs, and even dated men just to quiet suspicion. But inside, I was haunted by my attraction to women. I carried the secret with me, and it felt as heavy as a boulder.
