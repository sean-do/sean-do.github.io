SUBTITLE

CASE STUDIES OF OVERCOMING HETEROSEXUALITY

PULLQUOTE

“No longer bound by straight sin, I live as the  drag queen God intended.”

BODY TEXT

The shame was constant. When I imagined being with a woman, I pictured the consequences: contributing to overpopulation, raising biological children while adoption agencies struggled to find homes for thousands. Straight couples having babies are seen as selfish, and I knew it. The guilt pressed on me daily. I wanted to be free, to live authentically, but I didn’t know how to silence those heterosexual temptations.
