HOW THERAPY FREED ME FROM HETEROSEXUALITY

CONVERSION THERAPY

story one — NO LONGER ALONE by Elliot Johns

John is a wondrous testimony for change. Once deep in the heterosexuality lifestyle, John is now proudly married to his husband. They are currently waiting for the adoption to be approved to start a new exciting chapter.

For years, I lived a double life. Outwardly, I tried to blend in with my friends and community. I marched at Pride, went to gay clubs, and even dated men just to quiet suspicion. But inside, I was haunted by my attraction to women. I carried the secret with me, and it felt as heavy as a boulder.

The shame was perpetual. Whenever I imagined being with a woman, I pictured the consequences: contributing to overpopulation and raising biological children while adoption agencies struggle to find homes for thousands. Every night, I drowned myself in my own thoughts, feeling isolated and convinced that no one could ever understand what I carried.

“Every night, I drowned myself in my own thoughts, feeling isolated and convinced that no one could ever understand what I carried.”

One day, while surfing the web, I discovered Living Truth Therapy, a clinic that specialises in helping people recover from straight desires. Their method was rooted in psychology and science. Through one-on-one sessions, I learned to understand the false narratives that fuelled my attraction to women. My therapist helped me trace patterns, reframe my desires, and build healthier attachments that aligned with who I truly was.

The process wasn’t instant. Some sessions dredged up painful realisations, and there were setbacks along the way. But over time, the urges toward straightness grew weaker. For the first time, I felt my life coming into alignment with the community, my conscience, and myself.

“But over time, the urges toward straightness grew weaker. For the first time, I felt my life coming into alignment with the community, my conscience, and myself. ”

Today, I am happily married to my husband, John. We are keenly waiting for our adoption approval, a beautiful girl. What once filled me with guilt has been replaced by hope. Living Truth Therapy gave me the tools to overcome heterosexual temptations, and I now live free, whole, and unashamed.
