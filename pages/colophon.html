(ABOUT)

This publication was created as part of Communication Design studio — Future Communication at Monash University, under the guidance of Uriah Gray. It investigates the misinformation surrounding Conversion Therapy in the modern day. Instead of fact-checking, it creates a counter-narrative using the same persuasive techniques that made this cruel pseudo-scientific practice appear credible and authoritative in the first place. The appeal lies in the connection to the deeply internal feelings, emotions and personal struggles of its audience. Along with re-adapting and re-contextualising the editorial aesthetics, the counter-narrative urges readers to question how trust is manufactured through form as much as through content.

(DISCLAIMER)

This website does NOT promote or condone the practice of conversion therapy in any form. All contents excluding the last section The Truth of Conversion Therapy are purely fictional and satirical. The stories do not try to represent authentic experiences.
